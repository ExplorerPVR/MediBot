About MediBot

Founded in 2025, MediBot emerged from a vision to democratize healthcare access through technology. Our team of medical professionals and AI experts have created a platform that bridges the gap between patients and healthcare resources.

We combine medical expertise with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to provide reliable health information and recommendations. While our AI assistant doesn't replace professional medical advice, it serves as a powerful first point of contact for health concerns.

Our mission is to reduce healthcare disparities by making quality medical information accessible to everyone, regardless of location or socioeconomic status.
